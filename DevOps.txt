===========================================
            DEVOPS DOCUMENTATION
===========================================


1. **What is DevOps?**

DevOps is a combination of Development and Operations practices that improves collaboration, automation, and continuous delivery of software.  
Its goal is to deliver software faster, more reliably, and with higher quality using automation, CI/CD, cloud, containers, and monitoring.


2. **Why DevOps?**

- Faster development & deployment
- Automation of repetitive tasks
- Reliable infrastructure
- Improved collaboration between teams
- Better scalability using cloud
- Continuous monitoring & feedback
